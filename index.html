Kirk Hammett
Hammett replaced Dave Mustaine as the lead guitarist of Metallica in 1983 and has written some of the most iconic metal solos ever
Robert Trujilo
Despite following in the footsteps of bass legends Cliff Burton and Jason Newstead, Trujilo has left his mark in Metallica's most recent discography
